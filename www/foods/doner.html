Döner

Döner is a traditional Turkish dish made from seasoned meat (usually lamb or beef) that is cooked on a vertical rotisserie and served in a warm pita bread with various vegetables and sauces.

Ingredients

  • Meat (lamb or beef)
  • Pita bread
  • Vegetables (e.g., lettuce, tomatoes, onions)
  • Sauces (e.g., yogurt sauce, garlic sauce, chili sauce)
